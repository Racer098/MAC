Extra Information

Because each MAC address is unique and associated with only your device, it becomes a tool that may be used to monitor you. When you walk about, your smartphone searches for neighbouring Wifi networks to connect to, broadcasting its MAC address in the process. Renew London utilised garbage bins in London to track people's travels throughout the city based on their MAC addresses, which may subsequently be linked to a person's identification.

Tips and Tricks

Open your device manager on Windows, right-click on the network interface, choose properties, advanced, network address, and enter a custom value. Keep in mind that your ethernet adapter will almost certainly use a different MAC address than your WiFi interface, so adjust both. SMAC is a Windows software that facilitates this. It is also possible to employ tools such as Technitium, however it is not entirely necessary. Try to do this as frequently as possible, or configure your system to use a different MAC address each time your computer wakes up.

Apple IOS 

iOS 14 introduces MAC address randomization, so just activate it per network! If you don't have iOS 14, you can tether internet traffic through your PC using a faked MAC address.
